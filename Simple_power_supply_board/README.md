# Simple Power Supply Board

## Description
This is a simple power supply circuit board designed to provide regulated DC power output. The board includes voltage regulation, filtering, and protection circuitry to deliver stable power for electronic projects and embedded systems.

## Features
- Regulated DC power output
- Voltage filtering and stabilization
- Protection circuitry
- Compact design
- Suitable for powering microcontroller projects and small electronic devices
